Yosemite Weekend

A scenic three-night itinerary based at Rush Creek Lodge, designed around pool time, iconic valley viewpoints, family-style dining, stargazing, and easy-to-moderate hiking options.

View the itinerary Trip details
Half Dome sunset · Yosemite National Park
DatesJuly 17–20
Length3 nights
LodgingRush Creek Lodge
StyleFamily + scenic

Trip overview

This itinerary keeps the rhythm relaxed: lodge amenities on arrival, a classic Yosemite Valley loop on day two, and hiking options on day three before heading home.

Basecamp

Stay at Rush Creek Lodge, with onsite dining, pool time, and access to the Adventure Trail.

Signature sights

Tunnel View, Cathedral Beach, El Capitan Meadow, Sentinel Bridge, Cook’s Meadow Loop, and Valley View.

Hike options

Choose between the onsite Adventure Trail, Hetch Hetchy to Wapama Falls, or Mirror Lake Trail.

Photo sources include National Park Service public-domain photography and Rush Creek Lodge official photography.
